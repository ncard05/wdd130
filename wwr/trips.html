Currently Available Trips

Drops of Doom

This trip includes many different drops causing a fun bumpy ride. You will be screaming and laughing throught the whole trip.

Calm Cruise

This trip is perfect for those who are just here to relax and enjoy the scenery. A slow and steady ride to sit back and relax to. Has a variety of sceneries including forrest and caves. You might even spot an animal or two.

Rightfull Races

Aching for competition? This trip takes place in the widest part of our river and allows for multiple rafts to ride side-by-side. Three groups can race to see who can make it to the end of the river first.
